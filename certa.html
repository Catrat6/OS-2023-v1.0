The Secret Archives

A spot entitled “The Secret Archives” was created to celebrate the 30th Anniversary of CertaPro Painters. This piece was created by our team and shot at the CertaPro Painters headquarters in Audubon, PA. The Secret Archives was an idea we came up with to illustrate the history of the brand and how far they have come over the past 30 years in becoming the largest painting company in North America. The video was played at the National conference in California for all executives and heads of franchises across the U.S. and Canada.
