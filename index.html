Who We Are

Owl & Stag is a Philly-based production company specializing in the creation and execution of brand films for companies looking to beautify their brand's image and reconnect with their customers in a more memorable, organic way.

We are creatives first, with a team of directors, producers, designers, and post-production artists committed to developing engaging, multi-platform content for brands, agencies, and influencers.

We eat, sleep, and breathe digital content. Combining that with a love of storytelling we create pieces that last; that your company can be proud of down the road.

Simply put, we help brands stand out among their competition. We will work with you to consistently create strong content, and make you feel like you are putting your best foot forward with your public facing image.

Our Team

Phil Kwarta / Founder & Creative Director

Phil Kwarta is our award winning director whose multidisciplinary skill set has lef him through both the narrative and the ad world.


Phil’s ability to conceptualize ideas, direct talent, and stay cool under pressure makes him the ideal candidate for any and all undertakings for Owl & Stag.

Joseph Balestra / Director of Photography

Joseph Balestra is our Director of Photography and hails from our sister company, Anatome Studios.


Joseph's style is rooted in the play between light and shadows as he regularly tests the boundaries of what's possible with lighting while creating beautiful imagery that encapsulates the human spirit.

Jordan Shasteen / Art Director

Jordan “Gordon” Shasteen is the glue of all of our productions as he regularly catches what we may miss.


Jordan's keen eye and intoxicating energy make all of our sets a fun place to be because at the end of the day, this is the reason we do this; to make beautiful visuals and have a great time doing it.
